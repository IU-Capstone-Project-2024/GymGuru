V-up Crunch Technique:
  • Lie flat on your back on a comfortable, supportive surface, with your arms extended straight above your head and your legs fully extended.
  • Engage your core muscles and lift your arms and legs simultaneously, forming a "V" shape with your body. Aim to touch your toes with your hands.
  • Keep your legs as straight as possible and avoid bending your knees. Your movement should be controlled and steady.
  • Hold the peak position for a moment, then slowly lower your arms and legs back to the starting position, maintaining control throughout the movement.
  • Inhale as you lower down and exhale as you lift up during each repetition.

Position the phone horizontally so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track your repetitions and monitor your form for correct execution.

Recommended to perform 20 repetitions
