Lateral Raise Technique:
  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand.
  • Keep your arms slightly bent and raise them to the sides until they are parallel to the ground.
  • Pause at the top, then slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  • Exhale as you lift and inhale as you lower the dumbbells.

Position the phone horizontally so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track repetitions and check execution technique.

Recommended to perform 20 repetitions
