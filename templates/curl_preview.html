Bicep Curl Technique:
  • Stand straight with dumbbells in each hand, arms extended at shoulder level.
  • Raise one dumbbell, rotate your forearm, so it's vertical, palm facing your shoulder.
  • Lower to starting position and repeat with the other arm.

    • Position the phone horizontally so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track repetitions and check execution technique.

    Recommended to perform 10 repetitions per arm
