Crunch Technique:
  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  • Place your hands behind your head without pulling on your neck.
  • Lift your shoulders off the ground, engaging your core muscles.
  • Exhale as you crunch up, then inhale as you slowly lower back down.

Position the phone horizontally so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track repetitions and check execution technique.

Recommended to perform 20 repetitions
