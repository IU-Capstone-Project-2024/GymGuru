Forward Bend Technique:
  • Stand with your feet hip-width apart, ensuring your weight is evenly distributed across both feet. Let your arms relax naturally at your sides.
  • Take a deep breath in, filling your lungs completely. As you exhale, engage your core muscles and begin to bend forward from your hips, keeping your spine long and straight.
  • Maintain a slight bend in your knees to avoid locking them. Reach your hands down towards the floor or your toes, extending as far as is comfortable without straining.
  • Allow your head and neck to relax fully, hanging freely towards the ground. Avoid tensing your shoulders or upper back.
  • Hold this forward bend position for several deep breaths, feeling a stretch in your hamstrings and lower back. To return to standing, engage your core and slowly roll up vertebra by vertebra, lifting your head last.

Position the device so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track your form and repetitions.

Recommended to perform 10 repetitions
